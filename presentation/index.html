Capstone Project Presentation

InternTrack

Web-Based Internship and OJT Placement & Tracking System

Powered by OJTConnect — helping students find work through direct contact with companies and city offices.

Presented to: HR Department, City Hall

Title Defense

Proposed Title: InternTrack: A Web-Based Internship and On-the-Job Training (OJT) Placement & Tracking System

1 What it is

A responsive web platform that connects colleges, city offices, supervisors, and students to manage internships from placement until completion.

2 Why it matters

Replaces manual spreadsheets, scattered emails, and paper-based tracking with a single, real-time, role-based system.

Problem Statement

Colleges and city offices still manage OJT programs through manual processes, which creates serious bottlenecks:

  • Students struggle to find verified companies and city offices that accept OJT placements.
  • Supervisors and coordinators track attendance, reports, and requirements on paper or spreadsheets.
  • Communication is fragmented (text, email, calls), causing delays and missed requirements.
  • There is no centralized visibility of student progress, hours rendered, or completion status.
  • Approval of documents, reports, and attendance takes too long, affecting grades and deployment.

There is no unified, digital, department-based system that lets students discover openings, apply directly, and be tracked by their supervisors and coordinators throughout the internship.

Objectives

A For Students

Find OJT openings by department and course, contact companies directly, submit requirements, log attendance, and submit narrative/required reports.

B For Supervisors

Post openings, review applicants, verify attendance, evaluate performance, and approve student requirements and reports.

C For Coordinators

Monitor internship progress across departments, manage students and companies, and generate summary reports.

D For Administrators

Configure system settings, manage users, monitor audit trails, and back up critical data.

Scope & Limitations

In Scope

  • Role-based access (Student, Supervisor, Coordinator, Admin).
  • Department and course-based student division.
  • Company opening board, applications, and placement.
  • Attendance logging with hours computation.
  • Narrative reports and OJT requirements workflow.
  • Messaging between students and supervisors.
  • Dashboards, notifications, and analytics.
  • Exportable reports and audit trails.

Limitations

  • QR and selfie attendance are optional enhancements.
  • Email/SMS notifications are planned for future integration.
  • Mobile app is out of scope; system is responsive-web.
  • Offline mode is not supported.

Why the HR Department of the City Hall?

We chose the City Hall Human Resources Department as the primary client because it is the ideal testbed for a centralized OJT placement and tracking system:

🏛️ Central Hub

HR is the central entry point for all OJT applicants entering city government offices.

📋 High Volume

City Hall receives many OJT students from different colleges and courses every semester.

🤝 Multi-Department

Students can be deployed to various city offices (treasury, health, engineering, social services), requiring department-based matching.

🔍 Supervision Need

HR must track student attendance, required hours, and completion for endorsement and certificates.

💡 Real Impact

Digitalizing OJT management reduces paperwork, speeds up approvals, and improves public service delivery.

🎯 Feasibility

HR has clear authority, existing workflows, and a strong need for a transparent, auditable tracking system.

Proposed Solution

OJTConnect is the deployment module of InternTrack. It gives students a searchable board of OJT openings posted by companies and city offices, lets them apply directly, and then tracks their progress automatically once accepted.

1 Discover

Students browse openings filtered by department and course.

2 Connect

Students apply or contact the HR/supervisor directly via email.

3 Track

Once accepted, attendance, reports, and requirements are tracked in one place.

Key Features

🔐 Authentication

Login, register, role-based access, remember me, and secure session management.

📊 Dashboards

Role-specific dashboards with stats, progress bars, and quick actions.

📅 Attendance

Time in/out logging, automatic hour computation, supervisor approval.

📝 Reports

Narrative reports, weekly reports, supervisor feedback, and approval flow.

📁 Requirements

Document submission (MOA, medical, resume, etc.) with status tracking.

💬 Messages

Built-in messaging between students and supervisors.

🏢 Openings

Company/city office posting board, applications, and acceptance workflow.

📈 Analytics

Progress tracking, completion rates, and exportable reports.

🔔 Notifications

Real-time alerts for approvals, rejections, and deadlines.

Expected Benefits

For the City Hall

  • Centralized record of all OJT students deployed to city offices.
  • Faster verification of attendance, reports, and requirements.
  • Reduced paperwork and manual follow-ups.
  • Better coordination between HR, departments, and schools.

For Students

  • Easy discovery of verified OJT openings.
  • Direct contact with companies and supervisors.
  • Clear view of required hours, remaining hours, and approvals.
  • All documents and reports in one digital portfolio.

Summary

InternTrack with OJTConnect is a modern, responsive, and role-based web system designed to solve the OJT management problems faced by colleges and city offices. It helps students find placements, helps supervisors track progress, and helps coordinators and HR oversee the entire program efficiently.
