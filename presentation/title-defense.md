# Title Defense Document

## InternTrack: A Web-Based Internship and On-the-Job Training (OJT) Placement & Tracking System

---

## 1. Title

**InternTrack: A Web-Based Internship and On-the-Job Training (OJT) Placement & Tracking System**

**Subtitle / Brand:** *Powered by OJTConnect — Helping OJT students find work through direct contact with companies and city offices.*

---

## 2. Problem Statement

Colleges and universities that deploy students for internships and On-the-Job Training (OJT) still rely heavily on manual, paper-based, and fragmented processes. These traditional methods create several problems:

- **Difficulty finding placements:** Students struggle to discover verified companies and government offices that accept OJT students.
- **Slow communication:** Coordination between students, supervisors, and Supervisors is done through scattered phone calls, emails, and text messages.
- **Inefficient tracking:** Attendance, required hours, and submitted reports are tracked using spreadsheets or physical documents, making monitoring time-consuming and error-prone.
- **Delayed approvals:** Requirements, reports, and attendance records take a long time to be reviewed and approved.
- **Lack of transparency:** There is no single platform where all stakeholders can view internship progress in real time.

> **Core problem:** There is no unified, digital, department-based system that allows students to find OJT openings, apply directly, and be monitored by their supervisors and Supervisors throughout the entire internship period.

---

## 3. Objectives

### General Objective
To develop a modern, responsive, and role-based web application that streamlines OJT placement and tracking for colleges, city offices, and partner companies.

### Specific Objectives

1. **For Students:**
   - Find and apply to OJT openings by department and course.
   - Submit requirements, log attendance, and submit narrative/weekly reports.
   - Communicate directly with supervisors and view their progress.

2. **For Supervisors:**
   - Post OJT openings and review student applications.
   - Verify attendance, evaluate performance, and approve/reject reports and requirements.

3. **For Supervisors:**
   - Manage students, companies, and internship placements.
   - Monitor attendance and progress across departments.
   - Generate summary and analytics reports.

4. **For Administrators:**
   - Manage users, roles, and system settings.
   - Maintain audit logs and ensure data security.

---

## 4. Scope

### In Scope

- **User Roles:** Administrator, OJT Supervisor, Company Supervisor, and Student/Intern.
- **Authentication:** Login, registration, remember me, role-based access control, and secure sessions.
- **Department & Course Division:** Students are grouped by department and course, and openings can be targeted by department/course.
- **OJT Placement Module:** A searchable board of OJT openings, application submission, and acceptance workflow.
- **Attendance Module:** Time in/out logging, automatic hour computation, and supervisor approval.
- **Reports Module:** Narrative report submission, review, and feedback loop.
- **Requirements Module:** Document submission (resume, MOA, medical, consent, etc.) with status tracking.
- **Messaging System:** Private messaging between students and supervisors.
- **Dashboards:** Role-specific dashboards with statistics, progress bars, and quick actions.
- **Notifications:** Real-time alerts for approvals, rejections, and pending tasks.
- **Analytics & Reports:** Summary views of completion rates, attendance, and pending requirements.
- **Audit Trail:** Logs of logins, uploads, approvals, and edits.
- **Responsive Design:** Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

### Out of Scope / Limitations

- QR code attendance and selfie/GPS verification are optional future enhancements.
- Email and SMS notifications are planned for future integration.
- A dedicated mobile application is not included; the system is a responsive web application.
- Offline functionality is not supported.

---

## 5. Summary

**InternTrack** is a capstone project designed to digitize and simplify the entire OJT experience for colleges, city offices, and partner companies. Through its **OJTConnect** module, the system helps students discover OJT openings by department and course, apply directly to companies and city offices, and communicate with supervisors. Once accepted, the system tracks their attendance, reports, and requirements in a single, transparent platform.

The **HR Department of the City Hall** was selected as the client because it is the central gateway through which OJT students enter city government offices. The HR department manages a high volume of students from different schools and courses, deploys them to various city offices, and is responsible for verifying their attendance and completion. By using InternTrack, the HR department can reduce paperwork, improve coordination, and provide a better OJT experience for both students and city supervisors.

InternTrack is built with a modern web stack (React frontend, Express backend, and SQLite database), follows secure authentication practices, and is designed to be scalable, user-friendly, and ready for real-world deployment.

## 6. Why the HR Department of the City Hall?

| Reason | Explanation |
|--------|-------------|
| **Central Gateway** | HR is the entry point for all OJT applicants entering city government offices. |
| **High Volume** | City Hall receives many OJT students from different colleges every semester. |
| **Multi-Department** | Students can be deployed to treasury, health, engineering, social services, and other offices. |
| **Supervision Need** | HR must track attendance, hours, and completion for endorsement and certificates. |
| **Real Impact** | Digitalizing the process reduces paperwork and improves public service delivery. |
| **Feasibility** | HR has clear authority, existing workflows, and a strong need for an auditable system. |
