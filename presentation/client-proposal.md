# Client Proposal Brief

## InternTrack: OJT Placement & Tracking System

**Prepared for:** HR Department, City Hall  
**Prepared by:** Capstone Project Team  
**Date:** June 2026

---

## 1. Purpose of the Project

The purpose of **InternTrack** is to provide a single, modern, web-based platform that connects colleges, students, and city offices for internship and On-the-Job Training (OJT) management. The system solves the common problem of manual, fragmented, and slow OJT processes by offering:

- A digital **job board** for OJT openings posted by companies and city offices.
- A **tracking system** for attendance, hours, reports, and requirements.
- A **communication hub** for students and supervisors.
- A **dashboard** for Supervisors and administrators to monitor the entire program.

The ultimate goal is to make OJT placement faster, tracking easier, and completion more transparent for everyone involved.

---

## 2. Why the HR Department of the City Hall Was Chosen

### 2.1 Ideal Client Profile

The HR Department of the City Hall was selected as the client because it fits the ideal profile for this system:

- **It manages OJT at scale.** City Hall receives many students from different schools, colleges, and courses each semester.
- **It serves as the central entry point.** HR screens, assigns, and deploys OJT students to the appropriate city offices.
- **It supervises multiple departments.** Students may be placed in treasury, health, engineering, social services, planning, legal, and other offices.
- **It has clear accountability.** HR is responsible for verifying attendance, hours rendered, and completion requirements.
- **It benefits from digital transformation.** Replacing paper-based tracking with a web system reduces workload and improves accuracy.

### 2.2 Specific Needs of City Hall HR

| Need | How InternTrack Helps |
|------|------------------------|
| Track many students at once | Central dashboard with student list, progress, and hours |
| Assign students to departments | Department/course filtering and placement assignment |
| Verify attendance and hours | Digital time-in/time-out with automatic hour computation |
| Approve reports and documents | Online submission and review workflow |
| Communicate with students | Built-in messaging system |
| Generate completion records | Reports and analytics exportable for endorsement |

---

## 3. Proposed Solution

### 3.1 For the HR Department

The HR Department will act as both **supervisor** and **Supervisor** within the system:

- **Post OJT openings** on behalf of the City Hall and specific departments.
- **Review applications** from students and accept/decline them.
- **Track accepted students** through attendance, reports, and requirements.
- **Generate summary reports** for endorsement, certificates, or school coordination.

### 3.2 For Students

- Browse and search OJT openings by department and course.
- Apply directly to City Hall or specific city offices.
- Log attendance and submit daily/weekly reports.
- Upload required documents (resume, MOA, medical, consent, etc.).
- Communicate with HR supervisors and view their progress.

### 3.3 For Supervisors and Administrators

- Manage all users, students, companies, and city offices.
- Monitor internship status, attendance, and completion rates.
- Configure settings such as required hours, academic year, and departments.

---

## 4. Key Features Included

1. **OJT Opening Board** — Students find openings; HR posts and manages them.
2. **Application Workflow** — Students apply; HR reviews and accepts.
3. **Attendance Tracking** — Time in/out with automatic hour computation.
4. **Report Submission** — Narrative reports with supervisor feedback.
5. **Requirements Management** — Document upload and approval tracking.
6. **Messaging System** — Direct communication between students and supervisors.
7. **Role-Based Dashboards** — Different views for admin, Supervisor, supervisor, and student.
8. **Notifications & Analytics** — Alerts and summary statistics for monitoring.
9. **Responsive Design** — Accessible on desktop, tablet, and mobile.

---

## 5. Expected Outcomes

After deploying InternTrack, the City Hall HR Department can expect:

- **Reduced paperwork** — attendance, reports, and requirements are all digital.
- **Faster approvals** — supervisors can approve/reject items with one click.
- **Better transparency** — students can see their progress and remaining hours.
- **Improved coordination** — HR can monitor all city offices and departments in one place.
- **Data security** — user accounts, passwords, and audit logs are handled securely.
