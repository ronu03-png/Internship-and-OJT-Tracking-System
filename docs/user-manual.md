# User Manual

## InternTrack: OJT Placement & Tracking System

### 1. Getting Started

Open the application in a browser. The default admin account is:
- Email: `admin@interntrack.local`
- Password: `admin123`

### 2. For Students

1. **Register** or log in as a student.
2. **Find OJT** openings under the "Find OJT" menu.
3. **Apply** to a company or city office.
4. **Log attendance** daily under "Attendance".
5. **Submit** daily journals, weekly/monthly/final reports, and requirements.
6. **View** supervisor feedback and evaluation results.
7. **Check** announcements and calendar for updates.

### 3. For Supervisors

1. Log in as a supervisor.
2. **Post openings** under "OJT Openings".
3. **Review applicants** and accept or decline them.
4. **View your interns** under "My Interns".
5. **Verify attendance**, journals, reports, and requirements.
6. **Evaluate interns** under "Evaluations".
7. **Message** interns when needed.

### 4. For Supervisors

1. Log in as a Supervisor.
2. **Manage students** and **companies** from the sidebar.
3. **Assign students** to companies and supervisors under "Placements".
4. **Monitor attendance and reports**.
5. **Post announcements** and **calendar events**.

### 5. For Administrators

1. Log in as an administrator.
2. **Manage all users** under "Users".
3. **View audit logs** under "Audit Logs".
4. **Configure settings** under "Settings".
5. **View analytics** under "Analytics".

### 6. Keyboard Shortcuts (Presentation)

- **Arrow keys** / **Space** — navigate slides
- **F** — full-screen
- **T** — toggle thumbnail panel
- **Escape** — close thumbnail panel
