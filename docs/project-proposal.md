# Project Proposal

## InternTrack: OJT Placement & Tracking System

### 1. Project Title
**InternTrack: A Web-Based Internship and On-the-Job Training (OJT) Placement & Tracking System**

### 2. Introduction
InternTrack is a responsive, role-based web platform that connects colleges, students, and partner organizations (including the City Hall) for OJT management. It helps students find placements, and helps supervisors and Supervisors track attendance, reports, requirements, and evaluations.

### 3. Objectives
- Simplify OJT placement through a digital job board.
- Provide real-time tracking of attendance, hours, reports, and requirements.
- Enable direct communication between students and supervisors.
- Provide dashboards and analytics for Supervisors and administrators.

### 4. Scope
- Four user roles: Administrator, OJT Supervisor, Company Supervisor, and Student.
- Modules: auth, dashboards, companies, placements, attendance, journals, weekly/monthly/final reports, requirements, evaluations, announcements, calendar, messaging, analytics, audit logs, and settings.

### 5. Expected Outcomes
- Reduced paperwork and faster approvals.
- Centralized student progress monitoring.
- Better coordination between schools, students, and companies.
- A production-ready system ready for capstone defense.
